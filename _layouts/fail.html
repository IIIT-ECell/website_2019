About "Fail?"

"FAIL?" where incredible keynote speakers from diverse backgrounds will share their inspiring stories of setbacks, challenges, and how they transformed failure into a stepping stone towards their ultimate success.

Learn from entrepreneurs, military officials, scientists, lawyers, and more about their journeys through adversity and how they turned the tables to reach their goals. Gain valuable insights, actionable strategies, and a fresh perspective on failure's role in shaping success.

'FAIL?' is aimed at facing the moments of failure head-on, sharing them and most importantly improving by them. It is a celebration of moments we don’t generally share.

Our Speakers


Vartika Joshi

Lieutenant Commander Vartika Joshi, the fearless skipper of the historic all-woman Indian crew aboard INSV Tarini. Her story is one of courage, determination, and breaking barriers.

As the leader of the first six-member all-woman Indian crew to achieve the incredible feat of circumnavigating the globe on INSV Tarini, Vartika Joshi has shown the world what strength and teamwork can achieve. Through the vast oceans and challenging waters, she exemplified unwavering leadership, guiding her crewmates with resilience and a spirit of adventure.



Thangminlal Haokip

Mr. Thangminlal Haokip – a brilliant legal mind hailing from the esteemed National Law School of India University in Bangalore. His adept understanding of legal intricacies and unique perspectives are sure to command your attention. However, what truly sets him apart is his candid exploration of failures in his personal journey.

Thangminlal Haokip's odyssey in the domain of law carries profound significance. Through unwavering dedication and remarkable achievements, he garnered widespread recognition and admiration.
