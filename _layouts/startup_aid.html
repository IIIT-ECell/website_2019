About The Program

StartUp Aid is an intensive 40-day expedition designed to equip you with essential entrepreneurship skills, focusing on the key aspects of launching a startup.

Throughout the program, you'll take part in eight high-impact sessions, including:

  • ✦ Five Masterclasses - Learn directly from experienced entrepreneurs and industry leaders as they share insights and real-world experiences.
  • ✦ Two Mentoring Sessions - Validate and improve your ideas through the experiences of well-established entrepreneurs.
  • ✦ The Final Pitch - Present your startup to a panel of external investors and judges, who will decide the winners of StartUp Aid 2026.

To ensure hands-on learning, there are three small challenges, each associated with a masterclass, which are short tasks designed to reinforce key takeaways from the session. These challenges will be tailored to the insights shared by our guest speakers, giving you practical experience in applying what you learn.

Whether you're refining an idea or preparing to launch, StartUp Aid is your chance to gain real-world startup experience and take your entrepreneurial skills to the next level
