About Us

Entrepreneurship Cell, IIIT-Hyderabad is a non-profit organization run by students, and was established with the idea of creating, manifesting and guiding the entrepreneurial spirit in the student community across India and to encourage them to be successful entrepreneurs. As part of this mission, E-Cell, IIIT-Hyderabad wishes to reach out to colleges and engage students in events designed and conducted by the E-Cell that promises to invoke curiosity and enthusiasm towards entrepreneurship.

E-Cell, IIIT-Hyderabad conducts several events, seminars and workshops all through the year, both for students of and beyond our college for the purpose of cultivating an entrepreneurial spirit within youngsters. The very purpose of the events are to let young minds explore and demystify the idea of entrepreneurship.

The Campus Ambassador program has been designed in a way that puts skill development as a top priority. E-Cell wishes their ambassadors to be well-equipped to help advance the culture of entrepreneurship on their campus. The Campus Ambassadors, would be the face of E-Cell, IIIT-Hyderabad in their college. The skill-set that Campus Ambassadors would acquire through becoming a part of this program, would enable them to be better leaders and of course, give them an edge over their peers when it comes to their know-hows on entrepreneurship.
